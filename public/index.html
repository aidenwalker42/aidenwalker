ABOUT ME

Hello!

Thanks for visiting my Website! I am an entry-level Web Developer who feels more than confident to take on a career in this field. I have my coding fundamentals DOWN and could learn anything you throw at me no problem! Coding has always been something that has interested me since I was 14, It's the closest thing to being a wizard in the real world... You learn some esoteric words, and if you say them the right way, you can make things happen. Also the puzzle-solving aspect I really enjoy. After I graduated Highschool it took me a few years of soul-searching and contemplation to figure out what career path I wanted to take, It was brought to my attention way too late in life that Web Development Bootcamps EXIST so I decided to take the leap and enroll in a Full-Stack Web Development program through DevMountain. This was probably one of the greatest decisions of my life no joke, I absolutely loved it and thrived in the 16-week course. When you spend 12-14 hours a day for a few months on any given topic, that topic begins to become very clear. I have learned more in those 16 weeks than both my Highschool and College experiences combined. My learning ability, problem solving skills and willingness to work together as a team would make me a good candidate for any company in need of an Entry-Level Web Developer!

I hope that my GitHub and my most Recent Works speak for themselves in regards to what I am capable of in this field.
